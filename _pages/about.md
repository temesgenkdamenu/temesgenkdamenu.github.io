I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the [University of Kent](https://kent.ac.uk/) in the UK under the supervision of [Professor Shujun Li](https://www.hooklee.com/) and [Dr Alexandra Covachi](https://www.kent.ac.uk/engineering-and-digital-design/people/664/covaci-alexandra). My research focuses on cyber security and online safety education, particularly on empowering children and young people through effective and engaging approaches, including game-based learning methods.

I earned an MSc in Information Security and IT Management from [Edge Hill University](https://www.edgehill.ac.uk/) in the UK, and a BSc in Electrical Engineering from [Bahir Dar University](https://www.bdu.edu.et/) in Ethiopia. 

I have over twelve years of industry experience in technical and leadership roles. I worked at the [Information Network Security Agency](https://insa.gov.et/) Ethiopia at different technical and managerial positions, including leading the Cyber Security Governance and Management Division. I was also providing cyber security module at [Addis Ababa University](https://www.aau.edu.et/) in Ethiopia. I achieved different professional certifications including the Certified Information Security Manager (CISM) credential.
